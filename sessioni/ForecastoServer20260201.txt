  Architecture

  Core Features                                                                                                         
                                                                                                                        
  1. User & Authentication - Registration, login, JWT tokens, profile management                                        
  2. Multi-tenant Workspaces - Role-based access (owner, admin, member)                                                 
  3. Session-based Workflow - Chat-like sessions with:                                                                  
    - Undo/redo operations                                                                                              
    - Optimistic locking with version numbers                                                                           
    - Conflict detection and resolution                                                                                 
  4. Records Management - CRUD with areas (budget, prospect, orders, actual)                                            
  5. Transfer System - Move records between areas with history                                                          
  6. Projects & Phases - Project tracking with phases                                                                   
  7. Bank Accounts - Balance tracking and history                                                                       
  8. Cashflow Calculations - Running balances, inflows/outflows summary                                                 
                                                                                                                        
  Sign Convention                                                                                                       
                                                                                                                        
  - Positive amounts = inflows (income)                                                                                 
  - Negative amounts = outflows (expenses)
